# Tomato & Vegetable Soup with Halloumi
---A
4 tsp olive oil
---B
2 carrots, sliced
1 rib celery, diced
1 onion, diced
---C
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp each
    - ground cumin
    - ground coriander
    - curry powder
pinch each
    - cinnamon
    - hot pepper flakes
---D
1 can diced tomatoes
2 cups sodium-reduced vegetable broth
2 cups water
---E
1 zucchini, diced
---F
3 cups lightly packed baby spinach
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1 tbsp lemon juice
---G
4 slices (about 1/4-inch think) baguette
1/2 tsp za'atar
---H
half pkg (250 g) halloumi cheese, cut crosswise in 4 slices

A.  Preheat oven to 400 F.  In dutch oven, heat oil over medium heat

B.  Cook carrots, celery and onion stirring until softened (~5 min.)

C.  Add garlic, spices stirring (1 min.)

D.  Stir in tomatoes, broth, water and bring to boil, reduce heat; simmer (15 min.)

E.  Stir in zucchini; cook until tender-crisp (~2 min.)

F.  Remove from heat; stir in spinach, cilantro and lemon juice.

G.  Arrange baguette slices on rimmed baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil and za'atar.  Bake until crisp and lightly golden. (~6 min.)

H.  In nonstick skillet, cook halloumi over medium heat, turning once, until golden (~4 min.)

Serve: Top soup with halloumi and baguette.
