# Doenjang Jjigae
Bean Paste Stew
1/2 cup doenjang
4 garlic cloves
2 teaspoon gochugaru
1/2 pound flavourful cut of beef, such as short ribs, chuck or skirt steak, diced
1/2 cup half-moon slices of korean squash or zucchini
1/2 cup diced onions
2 scallion, cut into 1-inch lengths
16 ounces soft tofu, cut into small cubes
1 korean or anaheim chile, sliced thin, with weeds
1/2 pound littleneck clams, scrubbed and soaked in water for 30 minutes (optional)
6 cups beef stock (p. 197)


1. Mix the doenjang, garlic, and gochugaru into a paste in medium saucepan.  
Add beef, squash, onions, scallion, tofu, chile and clams (if using).

2. Stir the beef stock into the saucepan and bring to a boal over high heat.
Lower the heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes to marry the flavors.
Serve with rice.
