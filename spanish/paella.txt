# Paella
1/4 cup olive oil (medium heat)
---
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 red bell pepper (couple minutes)
---
1/4 lb chorizo sausage
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, diced
1-1/3 cup + 2 tbsp uncooked Arborio rice (2-3 minutes)
---
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup white wine
1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
1/8 teaspoon saffron
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper (bring to boil then simmer for 15 min.)
---
if rice is still uncooked stir in 1/2 cup more of chicken broth and cook for additional 5 min.
---
3 ounces squid rings
2 tomatoes, seeded and chopped
1 (10 ounce) package of frozen green peas (2 min.)
---
1 pound fresh mussels
3 ounces shrimp
(arrange seafood on top cover with foil and leave for 3-5 min.)
---
1/4 cup italian flat leaf parsely
1 lemon, sliced
garnish
