# Pickled red cabbage and pineapple slaw (p. 27 mexican mag)
- 1 cup shredded red cabbage
- 1/2 cup chopped fresh pinapple
- 1/2 cup shredded carrot
- 1/4 cup thinly slided onion
- 1/3 cup cider vinegar
- 2 tbsp pineapple or orange juice
- 2 tbsp fresh cilantro
- 1.5 tsp seeded finely chopped fresh jalapeno peppers
- 1 tsp honey
- 1/4 tsp toasted cumin seeds
- 1/4 tsp salt
