# Pistachio Cups (yields 24 cups)

## Chocolate outside
1/2 cup butter (112.5 g)
120 g honey
928 g cacao nibs
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

1.  add contents to blender noting weight
2.  blend until smooth
3.  transfer melted mixture to bowl


## Pistachio filling
2 cups shelled roasted pistachios
2 tbsp honey (165 g ?)
1/2 tsp green cardamom

1.  add contents to clean blender noting weight
2.  blend until smooth
3.  transfer to bowl

#Measure:
1. Total weight of chocolate = ..

2. Total weight of pistaccio filling = ..

## Cups

1.  calculate portions
    -> bottom layer = total weight of chocolate * 0.7 / 24
    -> top layer = total weight of chocolate * 0.3 / 24
    -> pistachio filling = total weight of filling / 24

2.  assemble in cups sprinkling top of each with a pinch of sea salt

3.  freeze until set (~1 hour)
