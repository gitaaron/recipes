# Cookie
3/4 cup (175 mL) shortening
1 cup (250 mL) packed brown sugar
1/4 cup (50 mL) molasses
1 tsp (5 mL) vanilla
1 egg
2 1/4 cups (550 mL) all-purpose flour
2 tsp (10 mL) baking soda
1 tsp (5 mL) ground cinnamon
1 tsp (5 mL) ground ginger
1/2 tsp (2 mL) ground cloves
1/4 tsp (1 mL) salt

# Icing topping
1/2 cup (125 mL) white baking chips
1 tsp (5 mL) shortening

1. Heat oven to 375ÂºF. Mix 3/4 cup shortening, the brown sugar, molasses, vanilla and egg in large bowl with spoon. Stir in remaining cookie ingredients. Shape dough into 1 1/4-inch balls. Place 3 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet.
2. Bake 10 to 12 minutes or just until set. Remove from cookie sheet to wire rack. Cool completely, about 30 minutes.
3. Heat white baking chips and 1 teaspoon shortening in small microwavable bowl uncovered on Medium-High (70%) 1 minute; stir. Microwave 15 seconds at a time until mixture can be stirred smooth. Drizzle over cookies.
