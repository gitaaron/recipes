# Mediterranean couscous salad
2 cups cooked coucous - cooled one cup uncooked
1 cup chopped broccoli florets
1 cup seeded chopped english cucumber
1 cup chopped red bell pepper
1 cup chopped fresh tomato
1 cup chickpeas drained and rinsed, also knows as garbanzo
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
Other suggested add ins: kalamata olives pepperoncini, red onion, roasted red pepper

https://laughingspatula.com/crunchy-mediterranean-couscous-salad/

Lemon Vinaigrette: 
1 clove large garlic - chopped
Juice of one lemon
3 tablespoons dijon mustard
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
3/4 cup extra light olive oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
