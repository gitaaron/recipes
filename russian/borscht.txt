# Borscht
#ingredients
- 2 pounds skinless chicken thighs
- 8 cups chicken or beef broth
- 2 pounds potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 inch cubes
- 1 (16 ounce) can diced tomatoes (not drained)
- 1 can tomato paste
- 3 large beets, peeled and shredded
- 1 large carrot, grated
- 2 tbsp dill, chopped (1 for pot and 1 for sour cream)
- horse radish
- 2 cups shredded cabbage
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1-2 tbsp ginger, minced
- 5 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- 3 tablespoons sriracha
- 1 bay leaf salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 can kidney beans with their juice
- sour cream
- perogies

#method

-> chop carrots, onions
-> peel potatoes and cube

-> boil broth with broth chicken thighs, potatoes and beets until soft and chicken is not red at the bone (pressure cook for 10 minutes)

-> meanwhile fry carrot and onions until soft

-> chop dill
-> slice cabbage
-> mince garlic

-> remove chicken and beats
    -> peel and slice the beats with mandolin

-> add other ingredients
    -> tomatoes, kidney beans, beets, carrot/onion, cabbage, sriracha sauce, onion, garlic, red wine vinegar, tomato paste, dill, and bay leaf, pepper


-> grate horse radish

-> mix sour cream with dill and horse radish

-> serve with bread & perogies
