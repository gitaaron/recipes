--- A
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground red pepper
1 teaspoon ground cumin

1 pound skinless orange roughy or red snapper fillets, cut into 1-inch cubes
3/4 pound Alaskan salmon fillet, skin removed and cut into 2-inch pieces 
3/4 pound red snapper fillet, skin removed and cut into 2-inch pieces 
8 ounces peeled and deveined uncooked medium shrimp

--- B
3 tablespoons olive oil, divided

--- C
1 medium onion, thinly sliced (about 1 1/2 cups) 
1 red bell pepper, cut into 3/4-inch pieces (about 1 cup) 
1 cup chopped green sweet pepper
other vegetables good for stew
6 cloves garlic, minced (1 tablespoon)

--- D
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 can diced tomatoes

--- E
1 tablespoon lime juice
1/2 cup snipped cilantro
Bottled hot pepper sauce (optional)


A. Coat fish with spice mixture

B. Heat oil in pan on med.-high for 1 min. Cook fish for 2 min. or until browned.

C. Heat remaining oil in a large dutch oven on med.-high for 2 min.  Add onion etc.. and saute for 6 min. or until veg. is soft.

D. Add salt, tomatoes and coconut milk. and bring to a simmer.

E. Turn heat to low or off and add lime juice and cilantro and additional hot sauce if required.
