# Fresh Apple Cake
From : Janet

---A
2 cups flour
2 cups sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda

---B
2 eggs
1/2 cup cooking oil

---C
5 cups thinly sliced apples (personally i prefer diced)

---D
chopped nuts
brown sugar

A. mix dry ingredients

B. beat oil with eggs and mix into dry

C. add apples 1 cup at a time

D. put in small caserole or loaf baking pan and sprinkle with chopped nuts and brown sugar

E. bake at 350 for ~50 min.
