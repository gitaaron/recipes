# Seared Coriander Scallops w/ Nori brown butter & asparagus
2 sheets toasted nori seaweed broken into small pieces
1 tsp sesame seeds, toasted
16 large sea scallops (~1.5 lbs)
1 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp sea salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
5 tbsp unslated butter
4.5 tsp fresh lemon juice
1.5 lb asparagus, trimmed
