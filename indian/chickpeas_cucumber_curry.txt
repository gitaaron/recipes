# Chickpea curry

--- A
1/3 cup cooking oil

--- B
12 oz onion (1 large), halved and sliced

--- C
2 Tbsp finely chopped ginger
1 tsp to 1 Tbsp chopped jalapeno (optional)

--- D
1 cup pureed tomatoes (2 medium)
2 tsp mango powder
1 Tbsp paprika
1 tsp turmeric
1 tsp salt

--- E
1 cup water (2 cups if serving with rice and not as salad)
4 cups cooked chickpeas

--- F
1 Tbsp dried green fenugreek leaves or 1/2 cup chopped cilantro (optional)

A.  Heat oil in a medium pot on medium-high for 1 minute.
B.  Add onion and sauté for 8 minutes, or until lightly browned.
C.  Stir in ginger and jalapeño pepper and sauté for 1 minute.
D.  Add tomatoes, mango powder, paprika, turmeric and salt, stir well and cook for 4 to 5 minutes, or until oil glistens on top.
E.  Pour in water and chickpeas, stir well and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover and simmer for 5 minutes, stirring once or twice.
F.  Stir in fenugreek (or cilantro) and allow curry to cool for 30 minutes.

# Cucumber
--- A
1 lbs long English cucumber, cut in 1/2 inch dice
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
2-3 Tbsp fresh lemon juice

--- B
1/2 to 1 tsp salt
1/2 cup chopped cilantro (optional)

A.  In a medium bowl, combine cucumber, black pepper, onion and lemon juice. Mix well and refrigerate, covered, until serving.

B.  Add salt and cilantro, toss and serve.
