# Baked brusell sprouts
- 1.5 lbs brussels sprouts, cut in 1/2-inch dice
- 2 Tbsp ground cumin
- 1/2 Tbsp salt
- 1/2 tsp crushed cayenne pepper
- 2 tsp mango powder
- 1 Tbsp cooking oil

1. preheat oven to 400 F

2. on a large baking sheet toss brussel sprouts with spice mix & oil

3. bake for 15 min. carefully stirring once after 6 min. (brussel sprouts should be dry but not wilted)


# Sour cream curry
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 1/2 cup buttermilk
---
- 1/3 cup cooking oil
---
- 1 cup chopped onion
---
- 2 Tbsp finely chopped garlic (6 medium cloves)
---
- 2 cups pureed tomatoes (4 medium, or canned is fine but not crushed)
- 2 Tbsp ground yellow mustard seeds
- 1 tsp ground cayenne pepper
- 2 tsp salt
- 1.5 Tbsp garam masala or ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground fenugreek seeds
---
- 3.5 cups water

1.  combine sour cream and buttermilk in a medium bowl

2.  heat oil in medium pot on medium-high for 45 sec.

3.  add onion and saute until golden brown (5 min.)

4.  add garlic and saute (2-3 min.)

5.  stir in tomatoes, spices saute until oil glistens (~5 min.)

6.  stir in water and bring to a boil

7.  reduce to low and simmer (5 min.)

8.  to prevent curdling, spoon about 3 Tbsp of hot curry into buttermilk/sour cream mixture

9.  pour mixture into pot, using wisk stir well and simmer until mixture reaches gentle boil (5 min.)

Top brussel sprouts with curry and serve immediately.
