#Coconut Vegetable Curry
---A
- 1/3 cup cooking oil
---B
- 1/2 tsp asafoetida
---C
- 2 cups puréed tomatoes (4 medium)
- 1 Tbsp salt
- 1 Tbsp ground cumin (optional)
- 1 Tsp ground coriander (optional)
- 1 Tbsp crushed cayenne pepper
- 1/2 Tbsp turmeric
---D
- 1 cup water
- 2 lbs celery root, peeled and cut in 1-inch dice
---E
- 1 large cauliflower (about 2 lbs), cut into medium florets
---F
- 1 cup coconut milk
- 7 oz rapini (without tough bottom stems), cut in 1/2 inch lengths

A. In a large pan, heat oil and med-high (45 s.)

B. Sprinkle in asafoetida and allow to sizze (30 s.)

C. Carefully add tomatoes, then stir and add salt, cumin, coriander, cayenne, turmeric. (Sauté 5 min. or until oil glistens)

D. Add water, stirring and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to medium, stir in celery root, cover and cook (5 min.)

E. Stir in cauliflower, cover and cook (5 min.)

F. Pour in coconut milk, the bring back to boil and stir in rapini.  Cook 1-2 min. while stirring then turn off heat.
