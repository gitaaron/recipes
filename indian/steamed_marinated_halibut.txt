# Steamed marinated halibut

##Group A
2 lbs skinless and boneless halibut
1/3 cup cooking oil
1 tsp salt
1 tsp ground cayenne pepper
1-2 tsp garam masala

1. mix 'group A' and marinate in fridge for 1-3 hours

2. Steam
    - bring water to boil
    - add fish  and reduce to medium-high for 4 minutes or until fish flakesa when gently poked with a knife
    - ensure fish does not overlap (cook in batches if necessary)
