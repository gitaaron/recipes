# Yam Tomato Curray (stove top right timer oven)

--- A
1.5 lbs yams (about 2 medium), unpeeled and halved
--- B
1/3 cup canola oil
--- C
5 Tbsp finely chopped garlic (15 medium cloves)
--- D
4 lightly packed Tbsp finely chopped ginger
--- E
4 cups pureed tomatoes (8 medium)
--- F
1.5 tsp turmeric
1 Tbsp ground black mustard seeds + 2 tsp ground fenugreed seeds or 1 Tbsp ground cumin + 1 Tbsp ground coriander
1 Tbsp crushed cayenne pepper
1 Tbsp salt
--- G
8 cups water


1. Bring pot of water to boil on high.  Boil yams on med. (20-25 min.) then let yams cool for 15 min.

2. Heat oil in large pot on med-high (30 sec.)

3. Add garlic and saute (2 min.)

4. Add ginger and saute (1 min.) 

5. Add tomatoes and stir.

6. Reduce heat to med. and add spices stirrin (5 min.)

7. Mash yams and add with water to masala stir well and bring to boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer (5 min.)
