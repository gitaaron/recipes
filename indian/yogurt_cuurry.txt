# Yogurt Curry

--- A, F
4 cups plain yogurt
1/2 cup chickpea flour
5 cups water
--- B
1/3 cup cooking oil
1 Tbsp cumin seeds
--- C
2 cups chopped onion
--- D
2 Tbsp chopped garlic
2 Tbsp ginger
--- E
1 tsp turmeric
1 Tbsp salt
2 tsp ground coriander
1 Tbsp crushed cayenne pepper
1 tsp ground fenugreek
--- G
15 fresh curry leaves
--- H
5 oz green beans (trimmed) (optional)
1 cup frozen peas (optional)
--- I
1/2 cup cilantro or 1 Tbsp dried fenugreek leaves (optional)

A. In large bowl whisk together yogurt, chickpea flour, and water until smooth

B. Heat oil on med.-high for 45 sec.  Add cumin seeds and allow to sizzle for ~45 sec. until they darken.

C. Immediately stir in onion and saute 6-8 min. or until golden brown for mild flavour or darker for toastier flavour.

D. Stir in garlic and saute for 1 min. then add ginger and saute for 30 sec.

E. Add turmeric - fenugreek seeds and saute for 1 min.

F. Stir in yogurt mixture whisking well to break up any clumps.

G. Bring to a boil, add curry leaves and reduce the heat to medium and cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. 

H. If you are using green beans and/or peas, add them in the last 5 minutes of cooking. 

I. In the final seconds of cooking, add cilantro (or fenugreek leaves), stir well, remove from the heat and serve immediately.
