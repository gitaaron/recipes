# Smashed Cauliflower & brussel sprouts with horseradish crumbs

1 head cauliflower, cut into small florets
1 lb brussel sprouts, trimmed and halved
1/2 cup olive oil
3/4 tsp salt and pepper
COMBINE and arrange as single layer on parchment-paper-lined baking sheet, bake in 400 F for 35 min.
2 tbsp salted butter
heat butter in pan over medium high and add vegetable mixter and mash
TRANSFER into casserole dish and top with crumb mixture and broil for 1 min.

# horse radish crumbs
20 saltine crackers, crushed
1/2 cup shredded gruyere or emmentaler cheese
2 tbsp longo's horseradish
2 tbsp salted butter, melted
toss together
