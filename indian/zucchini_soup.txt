# Zucchini soup

--- A
- 1/3 cup cooking oil
--- B
- 1 Tbsp cumin seeds
--- C
- 2 cups finely chopped onion (1 large)
--- D
- 2 Tbsp finely chopped garlic (6 cloves)
--- E
- 2 Tbsp finely chopped ginger
- 1 Tbsp chopped jalapeno pepper
- 1.5 tsp garam masala
- 1 Tbsp salt
- 1 tsp turmeric
--- F
- 2 lbs zucchini, cut in 1/2 inch dice
- 1/2 cup water
--- G
- 2.5 cups whole milk
--- H
- 1/2 cup chopped cilantro

A.  In medium pot, heat oil on med.-high (1 min.)

B.  Add cumin seeds and sizzle (30-45 sec.)

C.  Stir in onion (10 min.)

D.  Add garlic (1-2 min.)

E.  Add ginger and spice mix. (3-4 min.)

F.  Add zucchini and water and stir well (3-4 min. or until it is almost cooked to preferred texture)

G.  Add milk, stir and bring to light boil.  As soon as milk begins to boil turn off heat.

H.  Stir in cilantro.
