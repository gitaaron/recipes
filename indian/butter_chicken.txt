# Butter Chicken Sauce

--- A
1/4 cup cooking oil
--- B
2 to 3 Tbsp crushed garlic (6-9 medium cloves)
--- C
1 small can tomato paste
1 Tbsp paprika
1 tsp turmeric
1.5 tsp salt
1 tsp ground cumin (or 1 Tbsp if you are not using garam masal)
2 tsp garam masal
1 tsp ground coriander (optional)
1 tsp ground cayenne pepper (optional)
--- D
1.5 cups water
1.5 cups whipping cream


A.  Heat oil in medium pot on medium for 1 minute.

B.  Add garlic and saute for 1 min. until golden brown.  It will become slightly sticky but keep stirring.

C.  Add tomato paste (or crushed tomatoes) and stir well, then reduce the heat to low and add paprika, etc..
    Stir  well and saute for 4 min.

D.  Stir in water and cream, then turn off the heat and set aside until ready to serve.
