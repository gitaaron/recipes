# Roasted eggplant raita

- 1 lb whole eggplant (1 large) skin on
- 1/2 tsp cooking oil or more, for roasting
---
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
---
- 1.5 cups high fat plain yogurt
- 1/4 cup finely chopped green onions, white and tender green parts only
- 1/2 tsp finely chopped ginger
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp finely chopped jalapeno
- 1/2 cup chopped cilantro or 1 tsp dried mint
---

1. heat cumin seeds in small pan on high (1-2 min.)

2. pour seeds into mashed eggplant

3. add yogurt etc..

4. cover with plastic wrap and store for at least one hour up to 2-3 days
