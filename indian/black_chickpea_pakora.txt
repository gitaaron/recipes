# Black chickpea pakoras

- 1 cup dried black chickpeas
- 7 cups water
---
- 12 oz russet potato (1 large)
---
- 1 cup chickpea flour
- 1.5 cups finely chopped onion (1 medium-large)
- 3 Tbsp finely chopped ginger
- 1 Tbsp salt
- 1 Tbsp cayenne pepper
- 2 Tbsp ground coriander
- 2 Tbsp ground cumin
- 2 cups finely chopped cilantro
---
- 1 cup cooking oil (or more for a deep fryer)
