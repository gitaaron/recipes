# Eggplant paneer pate
---
2 lbs eggplant scored, coated in olive oil, roasted for 45-55 min. in 425 deg. F, cooled, peeled then mashed
---
1/3cup + 1 tsp cooking oil
---
2 cups chopped red onions
---
1/2 tsp asafoetida
---
1 lb tomatoes, chopped (3 large)
1/2 Tbsp + 1 tsp salt
1 Tbsp ground cumin
1 tsp turmeric
1 jalapeno pepper chopped
---
8 oz paneer cubed
---
1/2 cup finely chopped cilantro


1. Heat oil in large pot on med-high (45 sec.)

2. Saute onions (4 min.) or until sweating but not browned.

3. Add asafoetida stirring (45 sec.)

4. Stir in tomatoes, spices saute (3 min.)

5. Stir in eggplant + paneer (1 min.)

6. Mix in cilantro and serve immediately
