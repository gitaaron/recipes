# Chickpea croutons
1 15 oz BPA-free can chickpeas drained, rinsed and patted dry
1 1/2 tbsp oil
pinch sea salt
1/4 tsp ground cumin
1/8 tsp ground cinnamon
pinch ground ginger

# Soup
2 tbsp oil
2 stalks celery
1 carrot
1 yellow onion
1 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
3/4 tsp ground ginger and cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground cumin
pinch ground pepper
1 18 oz can unsalted diced tomatoes
1 tsp sea salt + additional to taste
2 sweet potatoes peeled and diced (about 1 lb)
6 cups low-sodium chicken or vegetable broth
2 cups diced or shredded cooked chicken
1/4 cup each chopped fresh cilantro and parsely
3 cups packed sinach leaves
1 lemon, cut into wedges
